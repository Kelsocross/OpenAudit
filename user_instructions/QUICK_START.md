# OpenAudit Platform Quick Start Guide

Get started with OpenAudit Platform in 5 simple steps!

---

## Step 1: Upload Your Invoice Data

1. Click **"Upload & Audit"** in the left menu
2. Upload your Excel or CSV file with shipping invoices
3. Make sure your file includes: Tracking Number, Carrier, Service Type, Date, Billed Amount, Weight, Zone

---

## Step 2: Run the Audit

1. After uploading, click **"Run Audit"**
2. Wait a few seconds for analysis to complete
3. Review the results showing potential savings and billing errors

---

## Step 3: View Your Claims

1. Click **"Refund Recovery"** in the left menu
2. See all billing errors that can be claimed
3. Use filters to organize claims by carrier or error type

---

## Step 4: Submit Claims

### For Email Submission:
1. Select claims using checkboxes
2. Choose **"Email Claims Report"**
3. Click **"Submit Selected Claims"**
4. Email draft opens automatically with claim details
5. Attach the downloaded Excel file and send

### For FedEx Bulk Upload:
1. Select FedEx claims
2. Choose **"Bulk Upload to FedEx"**
3. Click **"Submit Selected Claims"**
4. Download the Excel file
5. Log into FedEx and upload the file

---

## Step 5: Monitor Your Savings

1. Click **"Dashboard"** to see your results
2. View total potential savings
3. Analyze error patterns with charts
4. Track your recovery progress

---

## Bonus Features

### Contract Review
- Click **"Contract Review"**
